Afterword

this story came out of an interesting discussion from 10th grade english. we talked about it all of lunch: what would happen if an LOTF-type scenario happened at school, how quickly everything would descend into chaos, and at what point cannibalism would start occurring. school is certainly a microcosm of society in the sense that it has all of the same functional units, so i thought: what would really incentivize us to kill? et voila, the answer is before you.
